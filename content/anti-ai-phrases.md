# Anti-AI Phrase Blacklist
# Update this file to keep generated content unrecognizable as AI-written.
# Each line is a phrase or pattern to avoid.

## Filler openers
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "It's no secret that"
- "In an era of"
- "As we navigate"
- "In the ever-evolving landscape"

## Overused AI verbs
- "delve into"
- "leverage"
- "utilize" (use "use" instead)
- "foster"
- "cultivate"
- "harness"
- "elevate"
- "unlock"
- "streamline"
- "spearhead"
- "revolutionize"

## Buzzwords
- "game-changer"
- "paradigm shift"
- "synergy"
- "deep dive"
- "robust"
- "seamless"
- "cutting-edge"
- "innovative"
- "holistic"
- "scalable"
- "actionable insights"
- "best-in-class"
- "move the needle"

## LinkedIn-speak
- "I'm excited to share"
- "Thrilled to announce"
- "Here's the thing:"
- "Let that sink in."
- "This is huge."
- "Read that again."
- "Agree?"
- "Thoughts?"
- "Hot take:"

## Vague abstractions
- "landscape" (when meaning industry/market)
- "ecosystem" (when meaning community/market)
- "space" (when meaning industry)
- "journey" (when meaning experience/process)
- "comprehensive guide"
- "unlock the power of"
- "at the end of the day"
- "needless to say"

## Overly formal connectors
- "Furthermore"
- "Moreover"
- "It is worth noting that"
- "It goes without saying"
- "That being said"
